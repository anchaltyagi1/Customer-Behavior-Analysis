# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes customer purchasing behavior using **Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI**.

The project focuses on understanding customer spending patterns, subscription behavior, product performance, and revenue trends to generate actionable business insights.

## 💡 Key Insights

### 🏆 Clothing Generates Highest Revenue

The Clothing category contributes the largest portion of total revenue.

### 👥 Young Adults Are Top Customers

Young Adult customers generate the highest revenue contribution.

### 💳 Subscription Growth Opportunity

Most customers are non-subscribers, creating opportunities for subscription campaigns.

### 🔄 Loyal Customers Drive Revenue

Customers with repeated purchases contribute significantly to business revenue.

### 🎯 Discounts Influence Buying Decisions

Several products show strong dependency on discounts for purchases.
